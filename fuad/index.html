Ethiopian Wild Life Animals
The richness and variety of Wildlife of Ethiopia
is dictated by the great diversity of terrain
with wide variations in climate,soils,natural vegetation
and settlemen patterns.
Ethiopia contains a vast highland complex of mountains
dissected plateaus by the Great Rift Valley,
Which runs generally southwest to northeast
and is surrounded by lowlands,steppes , or semi-desert.
